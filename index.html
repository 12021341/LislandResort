FACILITIES

Swimming Pool

One of the highlight of this hotel is the large swimming pool surrounding the main building like a bluish lagoon reflecting the silhouette of the trees. What a pleasure to take a dip in its cool waters! You can relax in deckchairs, listening only to the sound of the birds that inhabit the little forest.

View Swimming Pool

Bai Marin Restaurant

Dine amidst a rustic setting in bai marin restaurant. We serve filipino and international cuisine, promotes healthy regional dishes like meat, vegetables, and fish. Try our delicious bulalo, sisig, crispy shrimp, and others.

View Bai Marin Restaurant

Lakay Akong's Bar

What a better way it is to celebrate in Lakay Akong's Bar. Meet friends and enjoy the high life.

View Lakay Akong's Bar

Cabanas

Lisland Rainforest Resort cabanas will offer you top comfort, cleanliness, privacy parking and security at affordable room rates! With Lisland's well trained staff offering top-class service you think you could only expect at pricey hotels.

View Cabanas

Urduja Pavillion

In case you wish to have a taste of entertainment fully you must go to the pavillion place site, the heart of ball room dancing, group functions and other activities.

View Urduja Pavillion

Conference Hall

The conference room offers a flexible space for meetings, receptions and presentations and has fully integrated press conference facilities. Catering can be provided either in the room for smaller meetings or larger events.
